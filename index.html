Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care

From routine check-ups to advanced cosmetic procedures, we offer a full range of dental services.

Preventive & General Dentistry

Dental check-ups, professional cleaning (scaling & polishing), gum care, tooth-coloured fillings & fluoride treatments.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, smile design & makeover, tooth reshaping, veneers & aesthetic restorations.

Restorative Dentistry

Root canal treatment (single sitting available), crowns & bridges (zirconia, metal-free options), post & core build-ups.

Dental Implants

Single & multiple tooth implants, fixed teeth replacement solutions, implant-supported crowns.

Orthodontic Treatments

Metal & ceramic braces, clear aligners (invisible braces), smile correction for teens & adults.

Oral Surgery

Painless tooth extraction, wisdom tooth removal, surgical extractions.

Pediatric Dentistry

Child-friendly dental care, preventive treatments for kids, cavity management & fillings.

Gum & Periodontal Care

Deep cleaning (gum therapy), treatment for loose teeth, gum disease management.

Emergency Dental Care

Tooth pain relief, swelling & infection management, broken or knocked-out tooth care.
